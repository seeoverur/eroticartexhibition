Art. Desire. Liberation.

EROS UNCHAINED is more than an exhibition; it is a multi-sensory journey into the world of eroticism as seen through the eyes of visionary artists. We believe in the power of erotic art to challenge conventions, inspire dialogue, and celebrate the diverse spectrum of human sexuality.

From captivating paintings and sculptures to provocative performances and immersive installations, our curated collection explores themes of intimacy, passion, and the beauty of the human form in a sophisticated, empowering, and inclusive environment.

A Global Legacy

Charleston is the latest stop on a journey that has captivated audiences across the globe. Our exhibitions are part of a tradition that has celebrated erotic art in cultural capitals worldwide.

In vibrant cities like London, our past events have become legendary, growing into full-fledged festivals of sensuality and creativity. These multi-day spectacles consisted of intimate workshops, fascinating talks by key speakers, stunning art displays, and prestigious awards ceremonies honoring emerging talent. The atmosphere was electric with live shows featuring mesmerizing strippers and cabaret performers, all complemented by unique market stalls where attendees could acquire art and artisan goods. We are thrilled to bring this legacy of artistic freedom and celebration to Charleston.
